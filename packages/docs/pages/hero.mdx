Hero section of your landing page. It contains a title, a short description and a call to action button. In this zeus kit, the CTA is placed at the top-right corner of the screen.

### Features

- `Title and Description` Craft a captivating title and concise description to highlight your business's key message and value proposition.
- `Trusted-by Component` [learn more](/trusted-by)
- `Waitlist Component` [learn more](/waitlist)
